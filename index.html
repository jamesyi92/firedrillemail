As the parent of two happy campers I feel passionately that summer camp should be a safe community. But too many camps are unknowingly running surface-level, problematic background checks that can put campers—and the camp—at risk. It doesn’t have to be that way!

That is why I’m honored to present Background Screening 101 at the ACA, NY & NJ’s Tri-State CAMP Conference in Atlantic City on March 14th. I hope you can join me. We’ll unpack what makes a good background check and share questions to ask screening providers, so that you can be sure that you are getting a legally compliant, rigorous check. You’ll leave armed to make good screening decisions for your camp.

If you can’t join me on March 14th, please check out my blog post on the subject for some tips, or our web site, and be in touch if we can be of help.
